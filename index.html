About me

Hello there!
I'm Tharuka, a passionate engineering student with variety of skills and experience. I have a sound knowledge in mathematics, computer programming and other related topics. I always try to keep up to date with the latest knowledge. Apart from technical skills, I'm very good at communication, teamwork and other soft skills. Outside work, I often love reading novels, travelling, and creating new stuff.

Education

Rahula College is my alma mater where studied until 2018. There, I followed physical sciences (Math, Physics, Chemistry) & passed GCE A/L with good results. Then I decided to study engineering.

Currently, I'm reading for my engineering degree at the Department of Electrical and Information Engineering in the Faculty of Engineering, University of Ruhuna.

Work

I have gained many experiences by joining AIESEC in University of Ruhuna, where I started my journey as a member, becoming an IR(International Relations) manager and later the HR manager. It was a really good experience in my life and I was able to develop a lot of skills.

Projects

I'm very passionate in creating new stuff using microcontrollers like Arduino, ESP8266, and ESP32. Also I have done some software projects like 'Chat Application', 'ESP8266 Web Server', 'File Backup Application', etc. Take a look at my GitHub profile for some projects that I have done.
